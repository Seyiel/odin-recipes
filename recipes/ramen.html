Ramen

This is ramen. or more specifically it's tonkotsu ramen. This dish originates from Japan

Ingredients

Ingredients for the meat
  • 1-2lbs of pork cutlet/belly, chicken thigh or stew beef
  • 3.5 tbsp of ginger
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3.5 tbsp garlic
  • 2 cups of Soy Sauce and 1/2 cup of water
  • Chili sauce
Ingredients for the soup
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 cup of chicken broth/stock or 1 cube of chicken bouillon
  • 2 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 1/2 inch of ginger
  • salt and pepper
  • Add desired vegetable. examples: cauliflower, kale, carrots, spinach
  • chili or chili powder
Rest of the ingredients:
  • 2 instant ramen noodles (without the packet flavoring)
  • 2 boiled eggs

Recipe

  1. Cook the meat
    1. Add the meat
    2. 2 cups of Soy Sauce
    3. 1/2 cup of Water
    4. Grate the ginger
    5. Mince the garlic
    6. Cut the serrano pepper into small chunks
    7. Chop the onion
    8. Add all ingredients above in a pot
    9. Set the heat to low and let it sit for an hour
  2. Cook the soup
    1. Add 500ml Water to a pot
    2. Oil
    3. 1 green onion Chopped
    4. 1 cube of chicken bouillon
    5. Chili powder
    6. Mince the garlic
    7. Grate the ginger
    8. Cut the broccoli, cauliflower, lettuce
    9. Add salt and pepper
    10. Set the heat to low and let it sit for an hour
  3. Boil the egg
    1. Heat up water
    2. Once it's boiling add in the eggs and wait for 6-8 min
    3. Remove the eggs from the boiled water and submerge them in cold water
    4. Peel the eggs
    5. Cut them in half
  4. Make the ramen
    1. While the meat and soup is cooking, 10 minutes before they are done start making the ramen
    2. Add water and the instant ramen noodles in a pot
  5. Presentation
    1. Scoop the meat and sauce and place in a bowl
    2. Scoop the soup and place it on top of the meat and sauce
    3. Add the ramen and add the halved eggs on top of the ramen
